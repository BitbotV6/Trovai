AI that works while you sleep.

Answer 5 questions. Destination, property type, budget, intended use, timeline. Trovai's AI builds your buyer profile in real time.

About Trovai

Where AI meets human expertise.

Trovai is an AI-driven property platform connecting Dutch and Belgian buyers with luxury properties in the Côte d'Azur and Curaçao. Our technology works around the clock — qualifying, matching and nurturing leads automatically.

We partner with Living on the Côte d'Azur (5,000+ listings, rated 9.9/10, 19 years expertise) and At Home Curaçao (full island coverage, Dutch-speaking team). Our service is completely free for buyers — the seller's agent shares their commission.
